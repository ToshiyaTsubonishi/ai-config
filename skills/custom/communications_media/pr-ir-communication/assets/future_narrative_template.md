# Future Narrative Generator (Chronicles)

## 1. Philosophy
In the AGI era, PR is not about reporting the past; it is about **forecasting the future**.
Use the "Chronicle" format to create a narrative bridge between the current state and the 2030 Vision.

## 2. Headline Templates
*   **Technological Breakthrough:** "[Company/Tech] achieves [Metric] ahead of schedule, powering [Impact]."
    *   *Ex: "Global Hashrate Alliance achieves 50 EH/s, powering sustainable fusion cities."*
*   **Policy Evolution:** "[Governance Body] ratifies [New Protocol], enabling [New Human Right]."
    *   *Ex: "AI Constitution v4.0 ratifies 'Right to Disconnect', ensuring human autonomy."*
*   **Symbiotic Milestone:** "First [Hybrid Event] occurs, marking the union of [Human Aspect] and [Machine Aspect]."
    *   *Ex: "First Board Meeting held with 50% AI Directors, resulting in record employee satisfaction."*

## 3. Writing Rules
*   **Date:** Always use exact future dates (e.g., "2029.11.05").
*   **Tone:** Factual, Optimistic, inevitability. Avoid "We hope to...", use "We have...".
*   **Metric-Driven:** Always include a specific number (EH/s, JPY, User Count) to ground the fiction in reality.
