# Strategic-Triangle Architecture Framework

## Overview
The Strategic-Triangle is the fundamental architectural pattern for all SBI AGI services. It ensures that every system is not just a calculation engine, but a symbiotic entity consisting of Intent, Execution, and Ethics.

## The Three Vertices

### 1. Brain (Intent / Strategy)
*   **Role:** The strategic core that defines "Why" and "What".
*   **Components:**
    *   **AGI Foundation Models:** Reasoning engines (e.g., Gemini Ultra, GPT-5).
    *   **Strategic Vector DB:** Storing SBI's wisdom and decision logs.
    *   **Intent Parser:** Translating vague human commands into executable strategies.
*   **KPI:** Precision of intent alignment (Did we do what the user *meant*, not just what they *said*?).

### 2. Body (Execution / Infrastructure)
*   **Role:** The physical and digital machinery that delivers "How".
*   **Components:**
    *   **Capital Engine:** Automated trading, lending, and settlement protocols.
    *   **Global Hashrate:** The compute power driving the AI.
    *   **Codebase:** The actual software (Next.js, Smart Contracts).
*   **KPI:** Latency (Time-to-Execution) and Zero-Friction.

### 3. Soul (Empathy / Ethics)
*   **Role:** The moral compass that ensures "Symbiosis".
*   **Components:**
    *   **AI Constitution:** Hard-coded ethical guardrails.
    *   **Empathy Engine:** Affective computing to understand user sentiment.
    *   **UX/Persona:** The "face" of the AGI (Warmth, Trust).
*   **KPI:** User Wellbeing and Ethical Compliance Rate.

## Application in Projects
When starting any project, map the components to this triangle:
- "Where is the Brain?" (Where is the logic?)
- "Where is the Body?" (Where is the infrastructure?)
- "Where is the Soul?" (How do we protect the user?)
