# Disaster Response Flow (Earthquake > Shindo 5+)

## STEP 1: Immediate Action (0-5 Minutes)
1.  **Protect Yourself**: Get under a desk. Stay away from windows.
2.  **Wait for Shaking to Stop**: Do not rush to exits.
3.  **Fire Check**: Any fire? -> "Fire!" shout and extinguish if small.
4.  **Open Doors**: Secure exit route.

## STEP 2: Evacuation & Assembly (5-30 Minutes)
1.  **Evacuate**: Move to designated emergency assembly point (Park/Open space).
    *   *Do NOT use elevators.*
2.  **Roll Call**: Managers check their team members.
3.  **Report**: Report status to [BCP Commander].

## STEP 3: Safety Confirmation (30 Min - 3 Hours)
1.  **Send Broadcast**: "Are you safe?" via Safety Confirmation System / Slack / Email.
2.  **Monitor**: Compile responses.
3.  **Family**: Instruct employees to contact family (use Disaster Dial 171).

## STEP 4: Decision Making (Day 1)
- [ ] Is office safe to return?
- [ ] Is public transport running?
- [ ] **Decision**: Send everyone home OR Stay in office (if transport paralyzed).
    *   *If staying*: Distribute emergency water/food.

## STEP 5: Business Continuity (Day 2+)
- [ ] Assess impact on business operations.
- [ ] Activate remote work protocols.
- [ ] Notify clients of operational status.
