# Annual Shareholders Meeting (AGM) Checklist

## Phase 1: Preparation (3 Months Before)
- [ ] Determine Record Date (基準日).
- [ ] Board Meeting: Decide Date/Time/Venue of AGM.
- [ ] Board Meeting: Approve "Agenda Items" (Directors election, Dividend, etc.).

## Phase 2: Notice (3-4 Weeks Before)
- [ ] Draft "Convocation Notice" (招集通知).
- [ ] Legal Review of Notice.
- [ ] **Send Notice**: Must send 2 weeks prior to meeting date.
- [ ] Internet Disclosure: Post on website.

## Phase 3: Logistics (1 Week Before)
- [ ] Venue Setup & Rehearsal.
- [ ] Prepare "Scenario" (Script for Chair/President).
- [ ] Prepare "Q&A List" (想定問答集).
- [ ] Secure Souvenirs (if applicable - *Trend is to abolish*).

## Phase 4: The Day (D-Day)
- [ ] Reception Desk Setup.
- [ ] Vote Counting preparation.
- [ ] **Run Meeting**: Opening -> Reporting -> Resolutions -> Closing.

## Phase 5: Post-Event
- [ ] File "Resolution Notice" (決議通知).
- [ ] Publish "Extraordinary Report" (臨時報告書) to EDINET.
- [ ] **Minutes**: Create AGM Minutes (Legal Requirement).
- [ ] Corporate Registry Change (if Directors changed).
